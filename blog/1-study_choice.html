    My study choice activities, my motivation for pursuing HBO-ICT at HZ and what I plan to do after this IT program.

    Study choice activities

    Before actually starting my study at HZ, I participated in several activities to be sure of my choice. These are:

    • HZ Open Day; a day where you can look around stalls of multiple different programs. You can also get explanations of what they do and what they teach.

    • Two walk-in days for HBO-ICT; I physically sat down in two lessons and saw how the lessons went for me.

    I was satisfied after doing these activities and felt HBO-ICT was a good fit for my interests and skills, so I chose to follow this program.

    After HBO-ICT, I plan to become a software engineer. Maybe, I could even be someone who works on game engines!
