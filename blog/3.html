Programming experience before HBO-ICT

    9 September 2025

    The experience in programming I had before starting at HZ's HBO-ICT program.

    What I did before HBO-ICT

    Before I began studying HBO-ICT at HZ, I studied 'Allround coworker IT systems and devices' at Scalda (an MBO school).

    We did a lot of device-oriented stuff, like working with servers and fixing computer problems. However, we did NOT do much with coding except very basic stuff. During my internship at Woongoed Middelburg in that school period, I experimented a lot with Powershell, even made a few very functional programs there that they were quite happy with.

    From that point on, my interest in programming began developing to a point where I semi-often messed around with Visual Studio Code and Python.

    During my last internship for Scalda, I was coding a bunch of stuff in Joint Research Center Zeeland and went faster than they anticipated there. Loek van der Linde, the data science lab manager there, suggested me to look into trying out HBO-ICT if I wanted to pursue my interest in programming. This is what I chose after the HZ Open Day and two walk-in days.
