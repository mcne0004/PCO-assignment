Here you will find answers to questions I had during my time at HZ so far.
    These questions here are provided for people to learn the various things that I didn't know at first.

    Questions I had and received the answers to are provided below...

    • Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    • A: Go to the URL hz.mynetpay.nl and log in. If needed, add money to your balance if needed to print your document (you need at least 5 euros). At the top of the page find 'Web Print'. On this page, drag and drop files that need to be printed and select upload. Now go to any HZ printer, log into it by scanning your HZ card. You will see your document(s) ready to be printed in the printing list.

    • Q: How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

    • A: Go to your nearest HZ printer and log into it with your HZ card. Place your physical document into the printer, with the face(s) of the page(s) you want scanned facing down. When these are scanned, you can then upload the scan, causing it to be sent to your HZ email address.

    • Q: How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

    • A: Go to MyHZ. Click the button saying 'Change personal menu' and add the option saying 'HZ Webshop'. Now you have a clickable shortcut in MyHZ to the webshop. Inside this webshop you can access various pages, each with their own 'Order' buttons.

    • Q: How can you book a project space?

    • A: Go to MyHZ. Click the button saying 'Change personal menu' and add the option saying 'Selfservice Portal'. Now you have a clickable shortcut in MyHZ to the Selfservice Portal. Click the shortcut. On this page, you will see the option to reserve something. Click that option and then click the option for reserving a project space. Proceed with the website's instructions from here.

    • Q: What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    • A: Park your car between the juridicial building and the library. This is located at Kousteensedijk 3 4331 JE Middelburg. Parking here isn't normally free, so make sure to ask at the JRCZ reception for a free parking ticket after parking your car there.
